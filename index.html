01 — Overview

ZVAR

A one-day interdisciplinary festival-constructor connecting progressive Ukrainian youth culture with the German creative scene.

Concept

Kyiv urban aesthetics meets raw German industrial architecture. Panels, zines, streetwear, live music, design, food culture and cross-cultural electronic music — fused into one living ecosystem.

Mission

Build an intellectual and cultural bridge between Ukraine and Germany through independent media, contemporary art, youth communities and charity.
